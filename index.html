I.

About

Coordinates · Education · Beam

I'm a maritime security analyst focused on the People's Liberation Army's surface and sub-surface fleet, the undersea infrastructure that ties the Indo-Pacific together, and the narrative side of how Beijing talks about Taiwan. My work has been featured or quoted in the Wall Street Journal, the Financial Times, The Diplomat, RUSI, Newsweek, and TaiwanPlus News.

At the Taiwan Security Monitor I lead the Analysis & Monitoring team, which runs the open-source surface/sub-surface fleet tracker, the Taiwan Strait Transit Tracker, and the Taiwan Affairs Office Statement Repository. Before that I directed TSM's Geospatial Intelligence and Visualization teams, where we built a detailed map of East Asian military and critical civilian infrastructure and correctly flagged predictive indicators ahead of the Joint Sword 2024B and Strait Thunder 2025 exercises.

I previously interned at the Department of the Navy's National Maritime Intelligence-Integration Office, where I worked port-security and Belt and Road maritime risk assessments and built out a global map of undersea cable landing sites. I provide OSINT and GEOINT trainings to DIA's Joint Military Intelligence Training Center and the China Mission Group.
